Welcome to Kathmandu Kitchen

Kathmandu Kitchen, where tradition meets taste. For over 20 years, we've been delighting diners in the Kathmandu Valley with a diverse menu featuring Tibetan, South Asian, and authentic Nepali dishes like our famous dal bhat. Our restaurant is a beloved destination for food enthusiasts from near and far, offering a warm and welcoming atmosphere for all who seek a true culinary adventure. Come and experience the flavors that have made Kathmandu Kitchen a local favorite for two decades. At Kathmandu Kitchen, we pride ourselves on serving up authentic flavors that transport you straight to the heart of the Himalayas. From our sizzling momos to our aromatic curries, each dish is crafted with care and tradition.

About Us

The Vision Behind Kathmandu Kitchen: Aavas Khatiwada

Kathmandu Kitchen wouldn't be what it is today without the vision and dedication of its founder, Aavas Khatiwada. Ten years ago, Aavas, driven by his hard work and determination, embarked on a journey to bring the authentic flavors of Nepal and beyond to the heart of Kathmandu. His foresight and belief in the power of exceptional cuisine have seen Kathmandu Kitchen blossom into a renowned restaurant, cherished by locals and visitors alike. Aavas friendly nature extends beyond the faces of his customers. He fosters a warm and supportive environment for his staff, valuing their contributions and fostering a sense of community within the restaurant. His understanding personality and commitment to creating a positive work environment ensure a dedicated and enthusiastic team, all working together to deliver the unforgettable Kathmandu Kitchen experience.
